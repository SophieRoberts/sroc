After The Event

What happens after the finish?

If the event's using e-cards then you will need to download your times. The download point should be clearly marked. You will be given a printout with your split times and final time.

Tip. Don't forget to return your e-card if you hired one!

If the events not using e-cards then there's nothing more to do.

Will there be any results?

Most clubs post provisional results at each event. The popularity of the web and the introduction of e-cards have meant that results are often available on the internet on a Sunday evening. Otherwise, results will be posted at a later date.

Tip. If results are being posted, then you might have to fill out a results envelope at registration to guarantee you receive a copy.

Is there anything else to be aware of?

If you've enjoyed yourself, keep a look out for future event details. It's quite common for clubs staging future events to leave flyers near registration or the results.
