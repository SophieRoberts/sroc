The Start

How do I find the start?

The route to the start is normally signposted and / or marked with streamers.

What's the start procedure?

Generally, you will be called up, 2-3 minutes before your start time. There may be blank maps available for you to look at.

. . . and when I start?

Tip. If the event's using e-cards, you will need to punch the start unit. This should be clearly marked. Normally there's a start official to point these things out.

If the maps are pre-marked you pick up a map from the correct box and you're on your way.

If you need to mark your own map there will be master maps at the start where you can copy down the correct course. Be careful to ensure you've copied the course correctly!

Tip. The triangle denotes the start, and the concentric circles denote the finish.
